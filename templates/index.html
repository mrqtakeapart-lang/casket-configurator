The Origin

Papa Wieber's Last Gift

Our family built Papa Wieber's casket by hand. It was not a sad task — it was the last act of love we could give him. The smell of pine shavings, a quiet garage, the whole family gathered at a workbench.

Fiat Casket Co. was born from that afternoon. A CNC-cut kit that makes the same thing possible for your family: a real solid pine casket, precision box-jointed, assembled prayerfully.

Fiat — Mary's word at the Annunciation. "Let it be done." A fitting word for the last gift you give someone you love.

By federal law, funeral homes must accept outside caskets at no surcharge. You are free to purchase from us and present your casket at any funeral home.
